About

Abdul Gaffar Mohammed, CFA

Corporate Finance Professional & Equity Research Analyst

I'm a CFA charterholder and corporate treasury professional with experience managing SAR 1bn+ banking facilities and executing SAR 2bn project financing deals. My expertise spans equity research, valuation, corporate finance, and strategic decision-making in Saudi capital markets.

I built decoded.finance to provide independent, rigorous analysis of Saudi IPOs and corporate finance topics. The platform serves as a resource for finance professionals, investors, and decision-makers who want institutional-quality research without the bias.

My approach is simple: numbers-driven analysis, practical frameworks, and no fluff. Every case study and blog post is designed to be immediately useful — whether you're a CFO evaluating an IPO, an investor analyzing a listing, or a student building your financial toolkit.
